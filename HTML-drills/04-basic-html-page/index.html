The PB&J ordered list!

  1. Find your bread
  2. Pull out 2 slices of your bread, and set them down on a plate
  3. Find your peanutbutter
  4. Find your jelly
  5. Find a butterknife
  6. Find a spoon
  7. Return to your plate with bread slices, and set everything you've gathered down.
  8. Take the lids of off the peanutbutter and the jelly
  9. Using the butterknife, scoop some peanutbutter out of the peanutbutter jar. Spread on one slice of bread.
  10. Using the spoon, scoop some jelly out of the jelly jar. Spoon onto the slice of bread that does not have peanut butter on it.
  11. Pick up the piece of bread with the jelly on it, and set it jelly side down on the piece of bread with the peanutbutter on it.
